The Story

I started freelancing in 1998 during my study course in communication science at LMU Munich. Being quite successful in freelancing and frustrated with the formalities of my university I teamed up with a partner and we founded Liquidlounge in the year 2000, an organisation dedicated to deliver rich multimedia content to the web.

Once Liquidlounge was born I decided to start a professional freelance career, resulting in never graduating in my university. Very early I focused on the Flash platform and became a specialist in the language ActionScript. With ActionScript evolving I started to learn and use more complex coding patterns like composition and inheritance also structuring my code into MVC patterns.

As the demand for CMS powered websites emergerged, I also specialized on delivering rich TYPO3 powered websites. I used jQuery to add the missing interactivity. But Flash was still heavily requested in countless online marketing campaigns. I was mostly contracted, whenever my clients could not complete a task with the animation timeline and code was needed.

In the end, one of my clients (Redblue Marketing GmbH) offered me a permanent employment to help establish their new born digital unit.
